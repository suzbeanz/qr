Uh oh, you just scanned a random QR Code! That can be dangerous!

🚨🚨🚨

QR codes are super convenient — but scanning from unknown sources can expose your device, your identity, and your data. QR Code scams are on the rise and it can be easy to get tricked.

⚠️ Scammers can replace trusted QR codes with fake ones that trigger dangerous actions.

QR Codes Can Do Scary Things, Like:

  • 🔗 Redirect you to phishing sites to steal your passwords, even if you don't take any other action!
  • 💾 Download malware or tracking apps without you knowing
  • 📩 Send or receive texts or emails from your device secretly
  • 💳 Trick you into fake payment pages and swipe your card info

Scan Smart & Stay Safe Out There

  • ✅ Only scan codes you recognize and trust
  • 🔍 Preview the URL (your phone usually shows it first) & make sure the original QR code hasn't been covered with a malicious fake!
  • 📲 Use a scanner app with security alerts built-in (Like TrendMicro)
  • 🚫 Skip codes on random flyers or stickers you find in public, like this one that could have scammed you!

Stay vigilant and spread the word! 👻
